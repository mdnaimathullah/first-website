Dam Tu Pata

Located just outside San Salvador, Dame tu Pata exemplifies the power of grassroots movements in animal welfare.

key facts

  • Founded: 2017
  • Focus: Rescue and rehabilitation of abandoned and injured dogs
  • Impact: Over 200 vulnerable dogs rescued and rehomed
  • Their Journey: Starting as a social media initiative, Dame tu Pata has grown from rescuing one dog per year to saving hundreds. Their story is a testament to the power of community engagement and perseverance in animal welfare.
