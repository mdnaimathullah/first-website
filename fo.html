Our Commitment to Transparency

As a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization (EIN: 92-1947921), we are committed to full transparency in our operations and use of funds. We believe that trust is the foundation of lasting change, and we strive to earn and maintain that trust every day.

At The Animal Alliance, we’re not just dreaming of a better world for animals—we’re creating it, one spay and neuter at a time. Join us in this journey of compassion, and together, we’ll write a new chapter in the story of animal welfare.
